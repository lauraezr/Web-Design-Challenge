Humidity

The image above shows the humidity seen at several degrees of latitude. As we can see, the highes humidity is is the northern hemisphere between the equator and about 70 degrees north. The density shown in the upper right corner of this scatter plot makes it easy to see that the distance from the equator affects the humidity in a different scale in the northern and southern hemispheres.
